Please Select a Suitable Condition

Need Help?

What is a malfunction?

Faults which are more technical or electrical are considered malfunctions.
E.g. unresponsive ticket machine, electronic display board glitch, train doors not opening

What is damaged?

Faults considered damaged are more likely to be physical defects, broken seats, torn cloth etc, wobbly table

What is dirty?

Faults which can be amended with cleaning products are likely to be considered dirty

Why do I need to specify a condition?

The condition helps us to prioritise more serious faults and identify the staff required to respond to the fault

How do I determine condition?

Please select the most applicable condition listed. If unsure, or the condition isn't listed, please provide more details later
